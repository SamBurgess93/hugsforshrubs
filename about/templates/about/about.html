A hobby born out of lockdown

Hugs for Shrubs are sustainably-made macramé plant hangers using 100% recycled cotton rope and materials from local suppliers. As the business was born in Donegal, the plant hangers are all inspired by the local landscapes and colours. There are two styles of plant hangers: Errigal and Eske named after two of Donegal’s most scenic spots. The perfect gift idea for those with green fingers or anyone looking for Donegal-inspired decor!

Why Hug for Shrubs?

Sustainable Packaging

100% biodegradable/recyclable packaging with a handwritten note perfect for personalised gift orders!

Donegal Designs

Inspired by the gorgeous local landscapes of Donegal: Lough Eske and Mount Errigal.

Support local

Using 100% sourced Irish materials, you're supporting not just my business but also my suppliers.

Meet the Maker

My name is Orla & I developed Hugs for Shrubs from a lockdown hobby into a small business in May 2021. Inspired by the local landscapes and colours where I spent many a lockdown walk, I'm delighted to showcase my crafts with you. You'll find Hugs for Shrubs plant hangers online, in selected shops in Donegal Town and I regularly attend markets in Dublin. Follow on Instagram for more updates!
